Cowork @ Smart Sixty Six

Photographers, We Want You!

Cowork @ Smart Sixty Six - Are you a photographer? Need an affordable place to shoot and work? Or just meet your clients? Do you want to network with other photographers? Are you tired of working alone at home or in Starbucks? If so, come and check out Pinellas County's coolest Cowork space. Cowork @ Smart Sixty Six. We're in the heart of Pinellas county, so we're easy to get to from anywhere in Tampa Bay. We have Fast, FREE, WiFi. And if you like to work at weird hours and sleep when everyone else is awake, that's ok with us too because we're 24/7. We've got two shooting bays, one with natural light and one off camera. And if you're doing boudoir photos or anything needing privacy we have privacy curtains too! Call Jane today!

So what are you waiting for? Come and Join our cool, shared workspace and our creative, entrepreneurial community of photographers, artists, entrepreneurs, small business owners, and freelancers. Prices start at $99/month! Check out our website and give us a call. Click on the contact us button above.

Our Cowork space is in a cool LOFT with 24/7 ACCESS and FAST, FREE WiFi. You can choose to have a dedicated desk or work at our communal tables. We have a conference room for private meetings, a kitchen with fridge, microwave, and Keurig, and spacious, comfortable workspaces within the Cowork Community. (If you want a private office we have those too! If you're an artist we have a drafting table, and easels. If you're a photographer, we have two shooting bays, one for natural light and one for Off Camera.)

Call Jane or check out our website - use the contact button above.

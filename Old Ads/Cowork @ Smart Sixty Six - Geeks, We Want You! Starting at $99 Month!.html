Cowork @ Smart Sixty Six

Geeks, We Want You! If You Work Alone, We Want You!

Cowork @ Smart Sixty Six - Are you a programmer, a graphic designer, a techie, a freelancer, a contractor, an artist, an entrepreneur, a small business owner, or a geek? Are you tired of working alone at home or in Starbucks? If so, come and check out Pinellas County's coolest Cowork space. Cowork @ Smart Sixty Six. We're in the heart of Pinellas county, so we're easy to get to from anywhere in Tampa Bay. We have Fast, FREE, WiFi. And if you like to work at weird hours and sleep when everyone else is awake, that's ok with us too because we're 24/7. So what are you waiting for. Come and Join our cool, shared workspace and our creative, entrepreneurial community of professionals, artists, entrepreneurs, small business owners, and freelancers. Prices start at $99/month! No Long Term Commitments!

We've got a cool LOFT for you to work in with 24/7 ACCESS and FAST, FREE WiFi. You can choose to have a dedicated desk or work at our communal tables. We have a conference room for private meetings, a kitchen with fridge, microwave, and Keurig, and spacious, comfortable workspaces within the Cowork Community. If you want a private office we have those too! If you're an artist we have a drafting table, and easels. If you're a photographer, we have two shooting bays, one for natural light and one for Off Camera.
